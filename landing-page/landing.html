Upload Timetable

Note for Mac/Chromebook users: The "Choose File" button may not work. Please drag and drop your ICS file anywhere on this page to upload your timetable.
  1. Log in to Sentral.
  2. Go to your timetable page.
  3. Click Export as ICS (usually at the top right of the timetable).
  4. Save the downloaded timetable file to your computer.
  5. To upload your timetable:
    • Click Choose File below and select your downloaded ICS file, or
    • Drag and drop the ICS file anywhere on this page.
  6. Click Upload Timetable to finish.
